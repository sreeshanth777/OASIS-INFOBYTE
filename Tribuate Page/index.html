Virat Kohli

The King of Cricket

Virat Kohli is one of the most dynamic and successful cricketers of his generation. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. He is one of the greatest cricketers of all time. Kohli is known for his aggressive batting style, exceptional consistency, and leadership qualities. Kohli has set numerous records and has been a source of inspiration for millions around the world.

Career Highlights

  • 76 international centuries, 29 in Test matches and 47 in ODIs.
  • Leading India to their first-ever Test series win in Australia (2018-19).
  • ICC Cricketer of the Decade (2010-2019).
  • Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000, and 12,000 ODI runs.
  • Scoring 70+ international centuries (as of 2024).
  • Winning the ICC ODI Player of the Year award three times.
